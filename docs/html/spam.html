SPAM

Junk or unsolicited emails, sent in bulk to a large list of recipients.


Why SPAM


  1. Sending bulk emails or messages is cheaper and fast

  2. With one email, sellers can advertise their products to large group of people

  3. Cyber criminals wants to lure you to collect: sensitive information, money, inject virus or to crash your system.
