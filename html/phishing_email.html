Do you know the red flags to look out in an email?

Email Phishing

Red Flags

  • From address: Does it look familiar? Did your school ever sends this kind of emails?
  • Hyperlinks: Mouse over on hyperlink, does link to address is different website than mentioned in from address?
  • Grammer or Spelling mistakes: Are there any bad grammer or spelling mistakes in the email content? (Food for thought: There are 3 errors in this email, did you caught them?)
  • Attachements: What type of files are attahced? (In general only .txt files are considered safe.)
  • Content: Is email content is asking you to share any sensitive information, check any pictures, download cool games etc. ?
